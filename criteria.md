### **Key Evaluation Criteria for the Semester Project**

* **Research Motivation:** Is your research question persuasive? What pain points does it solve, or what knowledge gaps does it fill? Why is this pain point important?
* **Literature Review:** Have you reviewed relevant classic or recent literature in HCI/HAX? What are the knowledge gaps within this literature? How do these knowledge gaps connect to your research motivation? Why is it important to bridge these gaps?
* **Research Objectives and Questions:** Are the research objectives clear and logically connected to both the motivation and the literature review? Are the research questions precisely defined and testable? Is there a clear link between the research questions and the objectives?

### **Prof. Kevin McGee’s 8 Fundamental Questions**

* What's the research problem/question?
* Is the problem/question significant?
* What's the contribution to knowledge?
* Is the contribution vital?
* Originality of the contribution.
* How do readers know it's original?
* Validity of the contribution.
* Is it appropriate for a specific discipline?
