# Research Proposal Prompt

## Context & Team Approach

We are a team that starts from concrete personal interests and then searches for relevant research to support them — interest-first, research-second.

---

## Initial Concept: Shared Garden (just an example)

Our starting idea is a **shared digital garden** where friends can:

- View each other's personal garden spaces
- Use **drawing** as the interaction medium — sketches become flowers or visual elements reflected in the shared garden
- Optionally extend to a **physical artifact** (e.g., a holographic display with buttons) that sits near you, lets you peek into friends' gardens, and lets others send drawings to your space

---

## Generalized Design Space

From the garden idea, we extracted the underlying design variables:

| Variable           | Description                                       | Example Values                                             |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| `<something>`      | The shared artifact or space people gather around | Garden, constellation, coral reef                          |
| `<a_way>`          | The interaction modality                          | Drawing, tapping with rhythm/beats, breathing, movement    |
| `<feel_something>` | The intended emotional outcome                    | Bonding, calm, curiosity, a reason to start a conversation |

---

## Core Design Values

1. **Creative & artistic** — the interaction and visualization should feel expressive, not utilitarian
2. **Physical over purely digital** — preference for tangible artifacts that combine software with calm, ambient presence _(calm tech)_
3. **Social but gentle** — not notifications or alerts; more like leaving traces for each other to discover

---

## Open Research Questions

- What existing HCI / tangible computing work explores artistic, ambient social connection?
- What interaction modalities (beyond drawing) feel expressive but low-effort?
- How do calm tech principles apply to shared physical artifacts?
